Hotdog Roll-Ups

Photo by Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package refrigerated crescent rolls
  • 8 slices American cheese
  • 8 hot dogs

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Unroll crescent roll dough and separate into 8 triangles.
  3. Place a slice of cheese on each triangle and top with a hot dog.
  4. Roll up each crescent roll around each hot dog.
  5. Place crescent-wrapped hot dogs on the prepared baking sheet.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, about 15 minutes.
