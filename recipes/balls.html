Spicy Sausage Balls without Bisquick

Photo by Allrecipes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spicy pork sausage
  • 1 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese, or more to taste
  • 1 1/2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 2 tablespoons coconut flour
  • 2 packages unflavored gelatin
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Combine sausage, Cheddar cheese, biscuit baking mix, coconut flour, gelatin, egg, and baking powder in a large bowl; mix well.
  3. Roll mixture into 1-inch balls and place on the prepared baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until browned and cooked through, about 20 minutes.
